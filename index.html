8 facts relevant to BCC

  1. I am studying here to get an associate's degree in web development. I am nearing the end of my second semester, so I'm about halfway through.
  2. I work at the help desk in the Sidney Silverman library.
  3. I like to start my work days with cold brew coffee from the downstairs cafe.
  4. My favorite place to go on campus is the cafeteria during sunset. I like the big windows. I feel like the rennovations have done a bit of a disservice to the original architecture of this building, which I admire. I also like the Science wing on the ground floor. I like the color scheme of that part of campus.
  5. Of the things I do for my job here I like doing cable management the most. It's fun and rewarding.
  6. Of the classes I have taken so far I enjoyed my intro to networking class from my first semester the most, I actually wish I'd been more attentive in retrospect.
  7. I drive to campus (I have to) but I'm a public transit lover at heart. Mostly because I think it's safer than driving, and I'm more accustomed to it after living in NYC for 8 years and then moving back in with my parents. When I move into a new apartment I'm going to try to find a place that is within reasonable public transit distance from BCC.
  8. I'm taking a Java class, originally I wanted to take the C/C++ class but it filled up. As someone who uses JavaScript and not Java - After learning a little Java, the more I think about it the more I see JavaScript as a sort of cursive of Java. It's like if Java and Python had a baby.
  9. Bonus fact: When I was helping out with some stuff I got some free hard drives. Most people think HDDs are obselete but they are actually easier to recover data frame when they break as compared to Solid State drives. Anyways, I've been using them to back up dvds.
